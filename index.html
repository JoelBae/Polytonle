HOW TO PLAY

Guess the three note chord in three tries.

Press CHORD to hear the chord.

Press PLAY C to hear a C4.

After each guess, the color of the notes will change to show how close your guess was to the chord.

Notes will be at least a whole tone apart and all accidentals are sharps.

New chord every day at midnight.

Reference

Green shows that the note is in the chord and in the correct order.

Yellow shows that the note is in the chord but in the wrong order.

Grey shows that the note is not in the chord.
